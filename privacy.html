Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 May 2026
Last updated: 2 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the operator of Xyven ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use the Xyven mobile application and related services (the "Service"). It is written to comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

If anything in this policy is unclear, contact us at xyven.app@proton.me.

1. Who is responsible for your data

The data controller is an individual operating the Service. Until a registered legal entity is established, all privacy-related correspondence should be directed to:

  • Email: xyven.app@proton.me

We have not appointed a Data Protection Officer because our processing does not meet the thresholds in Article 37 GDPR. The contact above handles all privacy questions.

2. What data we collect

2.1 Account data (when you sign up)

  • Email address
  • Display name (optional)
  • Password (stored hashed by our authentication provider, never readable by us)
  • Account creation timestamp and last sign-in timestamp

2.2 Trading profile data (when you set up a profile)

  • Trading style preferences (timeframe, instruments, risk tolerance)
  • Daily loss limits and risk-per-trade values you configure
  • Communication preferences (language, response style, emoji level)

2.3 Trade data (manually entered or imported)

  • Symbol, direction, entry/exit prices, position size, P&L
  • Stop loss, take profit, R-multiple
  • Strategy you assigned
  • Optional notes, screenshots, emotion tags
  • Timestamps and trading session

2.4 Connected broker data (only if you connect a MetaTrader account)

  • Broker login number and broker server name
  • Account balance and equity snapshots
  • Trade history retrieved through MetaApi (see Section 4)
  • We never receive or store your broker password — it is encrypted client-side before transit and stored encrypted at rest

2.5 Behavioural and journaling data

  • Mood, energy, stress check-ins you log
  • Reflection notes after trades
  • AI conversation history (your messages and the AI's responses)
  • AI-derived behavioural observations (strengths, weaknesses, tilt triggers)

2.6 Technical and usage data

  • Device type, operating system, app version
  • Crash reports and error logs (via Sentry, see Section 4)
  • Network status events the app needs to function offline

We do not collect precise location data, contacts, photos beyond those you explicitly attach to a trade, or social media identifiers.

4. Sub-processors

Supabase (database and authentication)

  • Stores account data, trade data, behavioural data
  • Location: EU region
  • Privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Anthropic (AI processing)

  • What they receive: your messages to the in-app coach, plus an anonymised summary of trading statistics (win rate, P&L ranges, recent emotions) — no email, no display name, no broker login, no individual price levels are sent
  • Purpose: generating coaching responses and behavioural analysis using the Claude language model
  • Data retention by Anthropic: processed in real time; not used to train Anthropic models when accessed via API
  • Location: US-based; SCC (Standard Contractual Clauses) and the EU–US Data Privacy Framework apply for transfer
  • Privacy policy: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy

MetaApi (broker connectivity)

  • Only used if you connect a MetaTrader account.
  • What they receive: your broker login number, server name, and the encrypted investor password (decrypted only momentarily within their secure environment to authenticate with the broker)
  • Purpose: establishing the read-only connection to your broker to fetch trade history and balance
  • Location: EU regions (London, Frankfurt) preferred; US fallback if EU regions are unreachable
  • Privacy policy: https://metaapi.cloud/privacy

Sentry (error tracking)

  • What they receive: crash reports and error logs, including stack traces and the email address of the affected user (for support follow-up)
  • Purpose: debugging and reliability
  • Location: EU region
  • Privacy policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

If we add or change a sub-processor, we will update this policy and notify you in-app or by email.

5. International data transfers

Some sub-processors (notably Anthropic) operate from the United States. Where data leaves the EEA, transfers rely on:

  • the EU–US Data Privacy Framework, and
  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, and
  • additional safeguards including data minimisation and anonymisation before transfer.

You can request a summary of the transfer safeguards in place by emailing xyven.app@proton.me.

7. Your rights under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data. To exercise any of them, email xyven.app@proton.me. We respond within 30 days.

  • Access (Art. 15): request a copy of the personal data we hold about you (a "DSAR" — data subject access request)
  • Rectification (Art. 16): correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure (Art. 17): request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten"). Closing your account triggers this automatically.
  • Restriction (Art. 18): ask us to limit how we process your data while a complaint is being resolved
  • Portability (Art. 20): receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON)
  • Object (Art. 21): object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Withdraw consent (Art. 7): if any processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority. In Finland this is the Office of the Data Protection Ombudsman (Tietosuojavaltuutetun toimisto): https://tietosuoja.fi/en/home

8. How we protect your data

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Database access is restricted by row-level security: every query is scoped to your user ID
  • Broker passwords are encrypted with AES-GCM before being stored; decryption keys are never accessible from the application code
  • AI requests are anonymised before being sent to Anthropic
  • We log security-relevant events (sign-ins, broker connections, password changes) and review them periodically
  • Personnel with access to production data are limited to the engineering team and bound by confidentiality

No system is perfectly secure. If a data breach occurs that may affect your rights, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours as required by Art. 33 GDPR.

9. Children's privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact xyven.app@proton.me and we will delete the account and associated data.
