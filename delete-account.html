Delete Your Xyven Account

You can permanently delete your Xyven account and all associated data at any time. This page explains the two ways to request account deletion.

Option 1: Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the Xyven app on your device
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm by typing your profile name
  5. Tap Permanently Delete

Your account and all associated data will be deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app, send an email to support@xyven.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Include the following information in your email:

  • The email address associated with your Xyven account
  • A brief confirmation that you wish to delete the account

We will process your request within 30 days, in accordance with GDPR and applicable data protection laws. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

What data is deleted

Permanently deleted:

  • Account profile (name, email, preferences)
  • All trade history and journal entries
  • AI coaching conversations and personalized insights
  • Analytics, statistics, and behavioral patterns
  • Uploaded screenshots and attachments
  • Strategy configurations and watchlists
  • Subscription history (after legal retention period)

What data may be retained

Some data may be retained for a limited period as required by law:

  • Financial transaction records — retained for up to 7 years for tax and accounting purposes (only applies if you had a paid subscription)
  • Anonymized analytics — aggregated data with no personal identifiers may be kept for product improvement
  • Security logs — retained for up to 90 days for fraud prevention

All retained data is anonymized or pseudonymized where possible and is protected under our Privacy Policy.

⚠️ This action cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will not be able to recover your trading history, journal entries, or any other data. We recommend exporting your data first if you wish to keep a copy.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or data handling, please contact us at support@xyven.app.
